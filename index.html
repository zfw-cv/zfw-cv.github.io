About me

This is Zhifeng Wang（汪智峰）， a 24-year-old second-year Computer Science master of the National University of Defense Technology in China. I am a student of (iGRAPE) Lab @ NUDT, and my supervisors are Prof. Kai Xu and Assoc.Prof. Renjiao Yi . Before this, I received a bachelor’s degree from Jiangxi Normal University. And worked closely with Prof. Aiwen Jiang,I founded and was in charge of the Mt-aistudio of Jiangxi Normal University, which conducts research in the areas of computer vision. My research interests include Computer Vision、3D Vision and Computer-aided Diagnosis. My long-term goal is to make intelligent machines trustworthy.
